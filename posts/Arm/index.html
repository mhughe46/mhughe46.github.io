Frankenstein Factory Arm
A Frankenstein inspired factory arm I created for a class project. The inside is a wood box housing the servo, servo mount, and shaft coupling connecting shafts from the servo horn to the arm through a bearing. The outside of the box is aluminum sheet metal which was deburred, hammered into shape, and screwed into the wooden frame. The metal was also scuffed with sandpaper and splattered with paint to give a more worn look. I sculpted the arm in Blender and 3D printed it before sanding, priming, and painting it.
