Hoppy the Penguin
A penguin named Hoppy who thinks he is a rabbit. This project was part of a semester long "first animatronic" class in which I came up with a character, created a turn around, function sheet, and model in Z brush before fabricating animatronic itself. Hoppy has three servos, one to control his hip bend, one for head side-to-side and one for wings and head up and down. I designed his skeleton in Fusion 360 in a modular way so that any broken parts could be easily replaced. His shell was sculpted in Z brush but separated in Fusion where I also added places for mechanical connection to the skeleton. I patterned his fabric by wrapping him in plastic wrap and tape which I cut along the desired seams. I then sewed along some of the seams and attached snaps to others so his fabric can be easily put on or pulled off.
